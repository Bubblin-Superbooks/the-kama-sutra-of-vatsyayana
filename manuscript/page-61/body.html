The following are the things to be done occasionally as diversions or amusements.

Holding festivals Social gatherings of both sexes. Drinking parties. Picnics. Other social diversions.

Festivals.

On some particular auspicious day, an assembly of citizens should be convened in the temple of Saraswati.
