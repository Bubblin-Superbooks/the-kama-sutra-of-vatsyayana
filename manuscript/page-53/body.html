1. A game, which consisted in repeating verses, and as one person finished, another person had to commence at once, repeating another verse, beginning with the same letter with which the last speaker's verse ended, whoever failed to repeat was considered to have lost, and to be subject to pay a forfeit or stake of some kind.
  2. The art of mimicry or imitation.
  3. Reading, including chanting and intoning.
  4. Study of sentences difficult to pronounce. It is played as a game chiefly by women and children, and consists of a difficult sentence being given, and when repeated quickly, the words are often transposed or badly pronounced.
  5. Practice with sword, single stick, quarter staff, and bow and arrow.
  6. Drawing inferences, reasoning or inferring.
