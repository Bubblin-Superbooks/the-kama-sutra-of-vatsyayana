By combining the above, the following six kinds of mixed results are produced, viz.:

(a). Gain on one side, and loss on the other. (b). Gain on one side, and doubt of gain on the other. (c). Gain on one side, and doubt of loss on the other. (d). Loss on one side, and doubt of gain on the other. (e). Doubt of gain on one side, and doubt of loss on the other. (f). Doubt of loss on one side, and loss on the other.

A courtesan, having considered all the above things, and taken council with her friends, should act so as to acquire gain, the chances of great gain, and the warding off of any great disaster.
