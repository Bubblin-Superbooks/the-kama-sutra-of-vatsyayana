  2. A woman who has been married in her infancy to a rich man, and not liking him when she grows up, desires a man possessing a disposition, talents, and wisdom suitable to her own tastes.
  3. A woman who is slighted by her husband without any cause.
  4. A woman who is not respected by other women of the same rank or beauty as herself.
  5. A woman whose husband is devoted to travelling.
  6. The wife of a jeweller.
  7. A jealous woman.
  8. A covetous woman.
  9. An immoral woman.
  10. A barren woman.
