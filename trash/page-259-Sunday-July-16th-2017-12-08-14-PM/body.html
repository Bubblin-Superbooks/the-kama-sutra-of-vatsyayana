Men who surpass their husbands in learning and good looks, in good quality, and in liberality. Men whose dress and manner of living are magnificent.

The following are the women who are easily gained over.

Women who stand at the doors of their houses. Women who are always looking out on the street. Women who sit conversing in their neighbour's house. A woman who is always staring at you. A female messenger. A woman who looks sideways at you. A woman whose husband has taken another wife without any just cause. A woman who hates her husband or is hated by him. A woman who has nobody to look after her, or keep her in check. A woman who has not had any children. A woman whose family or caste is not well known. A woman whose children are dead. A woman who is very fond of society. A woman who is apparently very affectionate with her husband. The wife of an actor. A widow. A poor woman. A woman fond of enjoyments.
